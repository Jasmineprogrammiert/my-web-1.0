Hello, my name is Jasmine ZENG. I was born and raised in Mainland China. I am a full-time Marketing undergrad at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, a part-time web developer and a language devotee.

As a person who values self-improvemnt and personal development to a great extent, I always strive to learn new skills & knowledge and aim for proficiency. That is why I aim to become a Full-Stack Web Developer, despite my initial goal of only creating a personal website. Furthermore, I am an avid language enthusiast. A language acts like a bridge that enables me to connect to people and their cultures on a deeper level. For this reason, I find it extremely fascinating. Right now, I am actively learning German and French after being fluent in English and Cantonese.

Besides the above activities, I also enjoy hiking, visiting arts museums, and exploring local neighborhoods for their food and cultures.
