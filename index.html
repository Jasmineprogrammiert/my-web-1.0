    NÇ HÇo/Hello/Nei Ho/Hallo/Bonjour

    Hello, my name is Jasmine Zeng. I was born and raised in Mainland China. In addition to being a Marketing undergraduate at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, I am also a web developer and a language enthusiast. As a person who values self-improvement and personal development to a great extent, I constantly strive to learn new skills and knowledge and aim for proficiency.

    My interests extend beyond academic studies to programming languages like HTML, CSS and JavaScript. My first foray into web development was in late 2021. Initially, I only intended to create a website showcasing my hiking experiences and photos. However, I soon realized that I really enjoy building websites and am always eager to learn more. At the moment, I am still working on front-end, but I plan to get into backend in late 2022.

    Furthermore, I am an avid language devotee. A language acts like a bridge that enables me to connect to people and their cultures on a deeper level. For this reason, I find it extremely fascinating. Right now, I am actively learning German and French after being fluent in English and Cantonese.

    Besides the above activities, I also enjoy hiking, visiting history and art museums, and exploring local neighborhoods for their food and cultures.
